Privacy Policy - Auto Scroll Extension

Last updated: March 2025

This privacy policy explains how our "Auto Scroll Extension" collects, uses, and discloses information when used.

Information Collected

Our extension collects the following types of information:

  • Anonymous usage data: We collect anonymous data about how you use the extension, such as frequency of use, features used, and selected settings.
  • Advertisement interaction information: Anonymous information regarding the display and interactions with advertisements in the extension is collected via Google AdSense.

We do not collect any personally identifiable information such as your name, email address, or browsing history on the websites you visit.

Use of Information

The collected information is used to:

  • Improve extension features
  • Analyze usage to develop better functionality
  • Display personalized advertisements via Google AdSense

Information Sharing

We share certain information with:

  • Google AdSense: To display relevant advertisements in the extension
  • Google Analytics: To analyze extension usage

These third-party services may use cookies and similar technologies to collect information about your use of the extension.

Your Choices

You can choose to uninstall the extension at any time if you no longer wish to have usage data collected.
